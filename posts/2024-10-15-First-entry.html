First Entry AI 2024-10-15

AI is here (duh) and has really taken it's place in almost every aspect of our lives - no matter if we like it or not. But is AI really.. well.. AI? The definition if "intelligence" is "he ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills".

Can an AI like CoPilot, Gemini, Claud and ChatGPT really do that? No.. they neither can or do that. What they really do is that they analyze your message and then look through their wast amount of data and finds what the most probable answer is. They actually don't know anything..

What you get as an answer from the AI is nothing more than a summary of our collective knowledge on the subject.
